---
title: Turning delivery work into a decision journal
date: 2026-04-12
summary: A work log becomes more useful when it captures constraints, rejected options, and why the chosen tradeoff held.
---

Most engineering writeups lose their value because they flatten decisions into outcomes.

## Better framing

Capture the following:

- the constraint that actually mattered
- the tradeoff that was accepted
- the operating rule created by the decision

That structure is more reusable than a timeline.
